Welcome to Singapore

Singapore, officially the Republic of Singapore, is a sovereign island city-state in maritime Southeast Asia. With a multicultural population and recognizing the need to respect cultural identities, Singapore has four official languages: English, Malay, Mandarin Chinese, and Tamil. Multiracialism is enshrined in the constitution and continues to shape national policies in education, housing, and politics. After early years of turbulence and despite lacking natural resources and a hinterland, the nation rapidly developed to become one of the Four Asian Tigers based on external trade, becoming a highly developed country; it is ranked ninth on the UN Human Development Index and has the fourth-highest GDP per capita in the world.
